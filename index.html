How it works
Four steps. Done in minutes.
1
Connect both platforms
Securely connect your old and new software. Read-only access on the source — we never touch your data without permission.
2
Preview your data
See exactly what will move: client count, appointment history, notes, memberships. Review a sample before anything migrates.
3
Run the migration
Click go. SwitchKit handles all field mapping, status merging, and dependency ordering automatically. Average: 4–8 minutes.

Download a full audit log of every migrated record. Rollback within 48 hours if anything feels off. No data ever lost for good.
